Country Meadows
subdivision


Country Meadows is located in the town of Greenville on Everglade Drive, just minutes from the Fox River Mall. A very rural setting with trees, wildlife and much more. Is a must for nature lovers! Each lot includes water, sewer, natural gas and electricity. Located just minutes from Hortonville schools.
